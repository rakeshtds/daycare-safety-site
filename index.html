Why Daycare Safety Bollards Are Essential

The Growing Threat

Every year, thousands of vehicles accidentally crash into buildings across the United States. Daycare centers and preschools are particularly vulnerable due to:

High Traffic Areas

Drop-off and pick-up zones create busy, congested areas where accidents are more likely to occur.

Vulnerable Population

Young children cannot react quickly to dangerous situations and rely on adults for protection.

Peak Times

Rush hour drop-offs and pick-ups increase the risk of distracted or hurried drivers making mistakes.

Building Proximity

Many daycare facilities are located close to parking areas and streets, increasing exposure risk.

Safety Fact

Vehicle-into-building crashes happen every 2.5 hours in the United States, with an average of 60 people killed and 2,600 injured annually.

How Bollards Protect Your Daycare

Physical Barrier

Creates an impenetrable barrier between vehicles and your building, stopping cars before they can cause damage or injury.

Visual Deterrent

Highly visible bollards alert drivers to potential hazards and encourage more careful driving in sensitive areas.

Traffic Control

Guides traffic flow and creates designated safe zones for children and pedestrians around your facility.

Protection Scenarios

  • Accidental Acceleration: Driver mistakes gas for brake pedal
  • Medical Emergency: Driver loses consciousness or control
  • Weather Conditions: Slippery surfaces cause loss of control
  • Mechanical Failure: Brake failure or stuck accelerator
  • Distracted Driving: Phone use or other distractions

Benefits for Your Facility

  • Peace of Mind: Staff and parents feel safer
  • Insurance Benefits: May reduce liability premiums
  • Professional Image: Shows commitment to safety
  • Compliance: Meets safety regulations and standards
  • Long-term Investment: Durable protection for years
