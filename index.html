Why Daycare Safety Bollards Are Essential

The Growing Threat

Every year, thousands of vehicles accidentally crash into buildings across the United States. Daycare centers and preschools are particularly vulnerable due to high traffic areas, vulnerable populations, and busy drop-off zones.

High Traffic Areas

Drop-off and pick-up zones create busy, congested areas where accidents are more likely to occur.

Vulnerable Population

Young children cannot react quickly to dangerous situations and rely on adults for protection.

Peak Times

Rush hour drop-offs and pick-ups increase the risk of distracted or hurried drivers making mistakes.

Building Proximity

Many daycare facilities are located close to parking areas and streets, increasing exposure risk.

How Bollards Protect Your Daycare

Physical Barrier

Creates an impenetrable barrier between vehicles and your building, stopping cars before they can cause damage or injury to children and staff.

Visual Deterrent

Highly visible bollards alert drivers to potential hazards and encourage more careful driving in sensitive childcare areas.

Traffic Control

Guides traffic flow and creates designated safe zones for children and pedestrians around your facility entrance.

Protection Scenarios

  • Accidental Acceleration: Driver mistakes gas for brake pedal
  • Medical Emergency: Driver loses consciousness or control
  • Weather Conditions: Slippery surfaces cause loss of control
  • Mechanical Failure: Brake failure or stuck accelerator
  • Distracted Driving: Phone use or other distractions
  • Elderly Drivers: Age-related reaction time issues

Benefits for Your Facility

  • Peace of Mind: Staff and parents feel completely safe
  • Insurance Benefits: May reduce liability insurance premiums
  • Professional Image: Shows commitment to child safety
  • Compliance: Meets safety regulations and standards
  • Long-term Investment: Durable protection for decades
  • Property Value: Increases facility value and appeal
