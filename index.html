The Unseen Dangers: Vehicle-into-Building Incidents

While we focus on what happens inside, a significant and often preventable risk looms just outside. The tragic **Richmond Hill daycare crash** is a stark reminder of how vulnerable our children are to vehicle-into-building incidents.

Over 100 crashes a day

According to safety experts, vehicles crash into buildings in North America over **100 times every single day**, resulting in thousands of injuries and fatalities each year. These are not rare occurrences.

Children are at high risk

A significant percentage of these incidents happen at storefronts, offices, and, most alarmingly, at **schools and daycares**. The lives of our most vulnerable are put at risk during common activities like drop-off and pick-up.

Lives are on the line

Every year, hundreds of children are tragically killed or injured in vehicle-related incidents in Canada and the U.S. These are not just statistics; these are children whose futures are cut short by preventable accidents.

How Bollards Enhance Safety

Vehicle Impact Protection

Fixed bollards create a physical barrier to stop or slow down out-of-control vehicles, preventing them from entering the building or playground area.

Visual Deterrent

Their visible presence acts as a strong psychological deterrent, guiding drivers and ensuring they maintain a safe distance from pedestrian-heavy zones.

Traffic & Pedestrian Control

They define safe pedestrian pathways, separate parking areas from play zones, and ensure a clear, controlled flow of traffic during drop-off and pick-up times.
