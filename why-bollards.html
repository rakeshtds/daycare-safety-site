The Critical Importance of Fixed Bollards

While many safety measures are in place, vehicle-into-building crashes remain a persistent threat to children's safety. Bollards are the definitive solution to mitigate this risk.

More Than Just Posts: A Foundation of Safety

Fixed, embedded bollards are not just simple metal posts. They are engineered to absorb and dissipate the force of a moving vehicle, providing a robust, unyielding barrier. Unlike other options, their permanent installation ensures they are always in place, offering continuous protection without the need for manual setup.

Key Advantages of Fixed Bollards:

  • **Maximum Impact Resistance:** Embedded in concrete, they are designed to withstand high-impact collisions, protecting the building and occupants.
  • **Permanent Protection:** They offer 24/7 security, eliminating human error or oversight in deployment.
  • **Cost-Effective:** While the initial installation is an investment, they require virtually no maintenance and provide long-term protection, saving costs and potential liabilities.
  • **Clear Boundaries:** They create a visible, physical separation between vehicle and pedestrian zones, reducing confusion and risk in high-traffic areas.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between fixed and removable bollards?

Fixed bollards are permanently installed in the ground and offer the highest level of protection. Removable bollards can be taken out to allow vehicle access, but this can create a security gap if they are not consistently used.

Will bollards look out of place?

Not at all. Modern bollards come in a variety of styles, finishes, and colors. They can be integrated seamlessly into the landscape, even enhancing the aesthetic appeal of your property.
